How to apply this warning

  1. Step 1 — Category: pick a top-level category (e.g. "Car Keys"). Stop here and the warning applies to every product in that whole category tree, including future products you add later.
  2. Step 2 — Subcategory (optional): pick a sub-branch (e.g. "BMW Remote Keys"). Narrows the warning to just that subcategory. The parent is automatically dropped on save.
  3. Step 3 — Sub-subcategory (optional): drill further. Each deeper pick replaces the broader selection.
  4. Step 4 — Specific products (optional): hand-pick individual products by name or SKU. Combine with categories or use on their own.

Tip: the deepest selection in each branch wins. Picking "Car Keys" then "BMW Remote Keys" stores only "BMW Remote Keys" — Audi keys in the same parent will no longer get this warning.
